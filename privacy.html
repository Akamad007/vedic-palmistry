Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Vedic Palmistry respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and stored when you use the app.

Information We Collect

When you use Vedic Palmistry, you may provide images of your left and right palms in order to receive a reading. The app may also store session-related information such as session history, generated readings, timestamps, and purchase status.

Palm Images

Palm images are stored locally on your device as part of your reading sessions. When you request a reading, the palm images and derived processed versions of those images may be sent to a third-party AI or language model service for analysis and generation of your reading.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used only to provide app functionality, including:

  • Processing palm images
  • Generating palmistry readings
  • Saving your recent session history locally on your device
  • Managing purchases and app access
  • Improving reliability and fixing issues

Local Storage

The app stores a limited number of recent reading sessions locally on your device. Session data may include palm images, processed image variants, timestamps, and generated reading responses.

Third-Party Services

If you request an analysis, your palm images and related request data may be sent securely to external AI or cloud processing services that help generate your reading. Those services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Purchases

Payments and in-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your full payment details.

Data Retention

Session data is stored locally on your device unless explicitly transmitted for analysis. You may remove sessions within the app, and deleting the app may remove locally stored content from your device.

Children’s Privacy

Vedic Palmistry is not intended for children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: https://akamad007.github.io/vedic-palmistry/

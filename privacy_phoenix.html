Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use the Flight of Phoenix mobile application.

1. Microphone Usage

Flight of Phoenix relies on your device's microphone as the primary game controller to detect breath. The audio data captured by the microphone is processed **locally on your device in real-time** to calculate aerodynamic lift. We do not record, store, transmit, or share any audio data to our servers or any third parties.

2. Analytics and Advertising

We use Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display advertisements within the application. AdMob may collect certain device information, advertising IDs, and usage statistics to provide relevant advertisements. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details on how they handle this data.

3. Game Center

The app integrates with Apple's Game Center to facilitate global leaderboards. Your high scores and Game Center identifier are safely transmitted to and managed by Apple. We do not maintain an independent database of your scores.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at bells.exhibit-0n@icloud.com.

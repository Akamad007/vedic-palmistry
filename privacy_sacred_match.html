Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SacredMatch ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS mobile application.

2. Data Collection & Usage

Camera & Photos: SacredMatch requires access to your camera and photo library strictly for the purpose of capturing images of palms and faces to perform compatibility analysis. The image data is sent securely to our AI models for real-time processing and is never permanently stored, sold, or shared with third parties.

Credit History: We use Apple's secure CloudKit infrastructure to synchronize your credit balance and reading history across your personal devices. This data is tied securely to your Apple ID.

3. Data Retention

We do not retain your biometric data (palm or face photos) on our servers after the compatibility analysis is complete. Your analysis history is saved privately to your personal iCloud account.

4. Third-Party Services

We leverage advanced AI models to process the compatibility readings. These services only receive anonymized images required for the reading and are contractually bound to not retain or use your images for any other purpose.

5. Your Rights

As all personal historical data is stored in your personal iCloud, you have full control to delete the application and its associated iCloud data from your device settings at any time.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@sacredmatch.app (or your relevant contact address).

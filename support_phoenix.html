If you are experiencing issues with Flight of Phoenix or have any questions, we are here to help.

Microphone Issues

The game requires active microphone access to function. If your Phoenix is not responding to your breath:

  • Ensure you have granted Microphone Permissions in the iOS Settings app under "Flight of Phoenix".
  • Ensure nothing is physically blocking your device's microphone.

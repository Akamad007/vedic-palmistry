Frequently Asked Questions

How do credits work?

You receive 500 free credits when you first install the app. Each reading costs 20 credits. Your balance syncs securely across all your Apple devices using iCloud.

Are my palm and face photos stored?

No. Your photos are only used for the real-time AI analysis. We do not retain, store, or sell your biometric data to any third parties.

What if the app crashed during a reading?

If a reading fails to complete due to a technical error, your credits are automatically refunded to your balance.
